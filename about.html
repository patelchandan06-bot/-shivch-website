WHO WE ARE

Craftsmanship Above All

Shivch Bespoke was founded on a single belief: that every man deserves clothing crafted exclusively for him. Based in Ludhiana — a city with deep roots in Indian textile heritage — we bring together master tailors and world-class fabrics to create garments that are as individual as the men who wear them.

From your first consultation to the final stitch, our process is unhurried, personal, and precise. We do not make garments in bulk. We make yours.
